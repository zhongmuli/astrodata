These files give the phot-SEDs of CCSN progenitors (preCCSN).

1. The phot-SEDs of preCCSNe with various metallicities are put in 8 files. Each file is for a metallicity. The metallicities are Z = 0.0001, 0.0003, 0.001, 0.004, 0.008, 0.01, 0.02, and 0.03.


2. Line 1 lists the parameters of each preCCSN. Parameters with mark "0" mean the intial values at zero age. Those with out this mark or with mark "f" mean the values at a time. Marks "1" and "2" denote the primary and secondary components of binaries. The units of age, mass, period, binary separation a, effective temperature, gravity, and luminosity are Myr, Msun, day, Rsun, K, m/s^2 and Lsun. Magnitudes are in mag.

Example:
           Z         age      mass10      mass20   Period0/d        ecc0       mass1       mass2       teff1       teff2       logg1       l
ogg2       logl1       logl2         kw1         kw2    Period_f    loga/r_s       ecc_f         FUV         NUV           u           g
       r           i           z           J           H          Ks          W1          W2          W3          W4


3. Then there are many lines that give the parameters including the magnitudes of preCCSNe. Each preCCSN takes a line.
